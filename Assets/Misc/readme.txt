collaborative world editing & hanging out - play this with friends online

*** CONTROLS ***

world
-----
arrows - move
enter - chat & log
(hold) space - show tile palette
(hold) shift - show walls
left-click - place tile
alt + left-click - copy tile from world

drawing (edit button in tile palette)
-------
left-click - draw
shift + left-click - flood fill
alt + left-click - copy colour from pixel
ctrl + c - copy tile to clipboard
ctrl + v - paste clipboard into tile

walls (hold shift in world)
-----
left click - toggle solid
this will stop working when unity's networking stuff stops being free
